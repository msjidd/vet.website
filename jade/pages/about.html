About Us

At Veterinary Services of Harbourside, we believe that pets are family. Located in the heart of Cordova, Cebu, our clinic is dedicated to providing compassionate and comprehensive veterinary care. Our team of skilled veterinarians and support staff brings a wealth of experience and a deep love for animals to every appointment. We offer a wide range of services, including preventive care, diagnostics, surgery, and emergency services, all tailored to meet the unique needs of your pet. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest technology to ensure your furry friends receive the best possible care. Our mission is to foster a lifelong bond between you and your pets through education, preventative care, and dedicated service. We are committed to creating a welcoming and supportive environment where you can feel confident in the health and well-being of your beloved companions. Thank you for choosing Veterinary Services of Harbourside. We look forward to serving you and your pets!
